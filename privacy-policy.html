Pickleball Speed Trainer does not collect, store, or share any personal information. We do not require an account, track analytics, or collect user data of any kind.

Any preferences or drill settings are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted externally.

The app may use limited system functions, including:

4. Children’s Privacy

The app is suitable for all audiences and does not collect information from users of any age.
